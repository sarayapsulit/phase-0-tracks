How to train a dog to sit

  1. Show your dog a small bite-sized treat to grab his attention.
  2. Push down his hind legs to be in a sitting postion then say "sit".
  3. Give him the treat. He should associate this action with as a good thing.
  4. Say sit again when your dog sits, give him the treat and tell him what a good doggy he is. (Prasise him without petting him.)
  5. Practice making him sit five times a day for five days in a row.
